About

About Us

Shree cleaning is a growing commercial cleaning services provider located in Sangli Maharashtra.

It is fully integrated cleaning services company that provides comprehensive, high quality, reliable cleaning solutions to Residential, Commercial and Corporate clients.

Our Services include housekeeping services, deep cleaning, residential apartment cleaning, corporate office cleaning, sofa cleaning and shampooing, window cleaning, chair cleaning, intensive cleaning, water tank cleaning and mattress cleaning for both residential and commercial clients.
